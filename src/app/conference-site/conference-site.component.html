Conference site

The conference will be held at the Faculty of Mathematics and Computer Science of Adam Mickiewicz University in Poznań, Poland (Street address: Umultowska 87, Poznań, Poland GPS coordinates: 52.46748, 16.92739).

The building is located at Morasko Campus in the Northern part of Poznań, accessible by tram, bus or car.

Getting to the conference site

You can easily access the Morasko Campus by taking the fast tram no. 12, 14, 15 or 16 from many points in the city. For example:

  • From the university guest house DS Jowita: go to the nearby Rondo Kaponiera (the Kaponiera Roundabout) and look for linies no. 12 or 14 (deep underground level) or line no. 15 (sub-surface), direction North (indicated ”Os. Sobieskiego”). After approx. 12 minutes ride get off at Os. Sobieskiego (this is the last stop).

  • From Vivaldi Hotel: go approx. 700 m to Słowiańska tram station(google maps link) and take any line (2, 14, 15 or 16), direction North (indicated ”Os. Sobieskiego”). After approx. 7 minutes ride get off at Os. Sobieskiego (this is the last stop).

When you get off at Osiedle Sobieskiego take a walk (North and then East) to the venue, approx. 750 m, 9 min (google maps link).
