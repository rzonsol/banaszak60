Conference fee and accommodation

The conference fee is 300 Euro (360 USD) if paid before July 31st and 350 Euro (420 USD) if paid after July 31th . It covers the registration, the conference kit, luncheons, coffee breaks and the entertaining events: the banquet and the concert.

The conference fee is to be paid via bank transfer into the Polish Mathematical Society account:
IBAN: PL 62 1500 1777 1217 7008 5391 0000
BIC/SWIFT: WBKPPLPP
Online payment 420.00 USD - Dotpay
Online payment 350.00 EUR - Dotpay
Please remember to write in the transfer description: "Banaszak 60, your name".

The accommodation is not covered by the registration fee.

If you do not wish to look for a hotel by yourself and would like to be lodged in University guest house Jowita (see the photos at https://dsjowita.home.amu.edu .pl/pokoje-goscinne/standard-podwyzszony/ ) please indicate that in the registration form (Special requests) and we will let you know how to make the reservation. The prices are:

  • single room – 100 PLN + 8% VAT

  • double room – 140 PLN + 8% VAT

  • double room single use – 110 PLN + 8% VAT

  • little apartment – 160 PLN + 8% VAT

  • little apartment single use – 120 PLN + 8% VAT

  • larger apartment – 180 PLN + 8% VAT

  • larger apartment single use – 130 PLN + 8% VAT

Thanks to the generosity of the Number Theory Foundation we are able to reduce the fee for 6 junior attendees who cannot obtain the funds from their department. The reduced fee is 50 Euro (60 USD). If you are eligible and would like to apply, please send e-mail to banaszak-60-aag@wmi.amu.edu.pl with an explanation why you need the reduction.
